Features
  • Load images in various formats, including jpg, png, and gif.
  • Choose from several options for selecting colors to be used in the pattern →more information.
  • Choose how large you want your final pattern to be (by choosing the size of squares to be imposed on your image →more information).
  • Edit your pattern (see the screenshot above) with tools including draw, change color, and fill region →more information.
  • Save your project and restore for later editing →more information.
  • Save the final pattern as a pdf.
